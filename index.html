CookingWithAI

Cooking With AI uses artificial intelligence to analyze images of food ingredients, identify them, and generate recipes based on them. Through image processing and language models, the system can recognize ingredients in a photo and suggest recipes that include them. This way, you can create delicious dishes tailored to the ingredients you already have at home!
